Why Link Your Dashboard to Instagram?

Sync Products

Automatically sync your products from dashboard to Instagram Shopping

Track Orders

Manage orders from Instagram DMs directly in your dashboard

Analytics

Get insights on which products perform best on Instagram

Product Tags

Automatically tag products in your Instagram posts and stories

Customer Data

How to Connect

1

Convert to Business Account

Convert your Instagram account to a Business or Creator account in Settings

2

Connect to Facebook

Link your Instagram to a Facebook Business Page

3

Set Up Instagram Shopping

Enable Instagram Shopping in your account settings and submit for review

4

Use Instagram API

Integrate using Instagram Graph API or third-party tools like Shopify, WooCommerce

Recommended Integration Tools

  • Meta Business Suite: Free tool to manage Instagram and Facebook together
  • Instagram Shopping API: Direct integration for custom solutions
  • Third-party Platforms: Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce with Instagram integration
  • Social Media Management Tools: Later, Planoly, Buffer for scheduling and analytics
