Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. Complex hardware issues may take up to a week, depending on parts availability. We'll always provide an estimated timeframe upfront.

Do you provide warranty on your services?

Yes! We provide warranty on all our services. Basic services come with 7-day warranty, Standard with 30-day, and Premium with 90-day warranty coverage.

Can you help with custom PC builds?

Absolutely! We offer consultation services for custom PC builds, including component selection, assembly, and setup. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Do you offer on-site services?

Currently, we operate from our workshop for quality control. However, we can arrange pickup and delivery for your convenience. On-site consultation is available for larger projects.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and most major credit cards. Payment is typically collected upon completion of the service, and we provide detailed invoices for all work performed.
